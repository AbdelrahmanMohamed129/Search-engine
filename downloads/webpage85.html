Who is Khan Academy?
Khan Academy is a nonprofit that wants to make education free and accessible for all. By joining Khan Labs, you can help us develop new features that will empower hundreds of millions of learners around the world! You'll test and provide feedback on new learning tools to help shape the future of learning.
Frequently asked questions
What's the time commitment for being a Khanmigo tester?
We are excited to start testing with you. There is no specific requirement for the amount of time you spend testing Khanmigo. You are welcome to test it as often or as little as youd like while you are enrolled. During the testing period, we will be sending you emails that ask for your feedback, but responding to those emails is completely optional.
How soon will I know if I got accepted to test Khanmigo? Can I check on my waitlist status?
We will be opening up seats for Khanmigo in a controlled environment to ensure there is enough capacity to handle the usage. We will send you an email as soon as you are accepted. Once you join the waitlist, you can come back to this page to check your status. Note: you will need to be logged in to see your status.
How do I sign up my children to test Khanmigo?
We are thrilled you want to have your children test Khanmigo. Sign up for the waitlist, and once selected, you'll have the option to enable Khanmigo access for your children.
Why arent you releasing Khanmigo to the public?
During this pilot phase, we are looking for valuable user feedback from people like you to help us shape Khanmigo before a public release.
Why is there a waitlist to test Khanmigo?
We have limited capacity to support testing while Khanmigo is being developed.
Why is there a donation to test Khanmigo?
Khan Academy is a small nonprofit funded by donations. Each interaction with Khanmigo incurs costs, and we need to expend significant resources to develop, test, and ensure safety of these new features. As Khanmigo costs significantly more than other Khan Academy features, we ask for monthly donations to offset expenses. Your contribution helps us develop a tool that could revolutionize education for millions, while granting you access to a groundbreaking learning experience.

If you don't meet the Khanmigo testing requirements (ages 18+ in the United States) or cant make a monthly donation, we'd love to have you subscribe to the Khan Labs newsletter for updates about AI and future testing opportunities! We understand this makes early access to Khanmigo prohibitive for some people. Rest assured that bringing down this cost and making Khanmigo more accessible to a broader audience is something that everyone on the team wants to happen.
Are people who donate the only ones who can test Khanmigo?
No. We have a pilot program where we are inviting approximately 1,000 teachers, students, and school district administrators to test and provide feedback. We understand a donation might not be possible for everyone. We encourage you to subscribe to the Khan Labs newsletter.
How will I find out about new features to test?
As we roll out new features to test in Khan Labs, we will be updating this page, sending out emails to all current testers, and sharing information with our larger Khan Academy community via email and social media. If you want to be the first to know, subscribe to our Khan Labs newsletter.
