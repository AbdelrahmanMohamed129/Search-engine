Frequently Asked Questions

What’s the difference between Ring Protect Basic and Ring Protect Plus?

Ring Protect Basic covers one Ring Video Doorbell or Security Camera for only 3.99 €/month or 39.99 €/year. It records all the videos captured by your Ring device and saves them to your account. You can then review all your videos at your convenience, download to save them for later and share them with friends, family and neighbours.

Ring Protect Plus covers all Ring Video Doorbells and Security Cameras at a single location for only £8/month or £80/year. It records all the videos captured by your Ring doorbell and/or cam and saves them to your account. You can then review your videos at your convenience, download those you want to save them for later or, if you so choose, share them with your friends, family and neighbours. If you have a Ring Alarm, with Protect Plus you can opt in to Assisted Monitoring, receive automated calls when your Ring Alarm detects a security issue. You’ll also get extended warranties for every eligible device for as long as your Protect Plus plan is in place.

Do I need Ring Protect to use my Ring device?

No. You can still use Ring doorbells and cams to watch over your home from anywhere, even without a subscription to Ring Protect. Without Ring Protect, you’ll still receive real-time notifications when anyone comes to your door, and you can answer the notification to see, hear and speak to visitors in real time right from your mobile device, from anywhere. However, without a subscription to Ring Protect, you won’t be able to review any videos that you missed in real time, and you won’t be able to save your videos or share them via the Ring app.

What devices are eligible for Ring Protect?

Ring Protect is available for all Ring Video Doorbells and Security Cameras. All devices come with a free 30-day trial of Ring Protect so you can explore all of the features and benefits. After setting up your Ring device, you’ll have the option to subscribe to Ring Protect. If you decide to subscribe to Ring Protect during your free trial, you’ll be charged after your trial ends.

Can I try Ring Protect before I subscribe?

Yes. A free 30-day trial begins as soon as you set up your Ring Video Doorbell or Security Camera.

How do I subscribe to Ring Protect?

First, you’ll need to sign into your Ring account on Ring.com. Then select “My Plans? from the dropdown menu at the top right. Next, choose the device you want to cover and follow the steps to add more devices. Then, select your preferred plan and add payment info needed to complete your purchase. For more detailed instructions, have a look at our video.

What happens if I add a new Ring device?

If you’re subscribed to Ring Protect Plus, all Ring devices registered at a single location will be covered automatically. If you’re subscribed to Ring Protect Basic and want your new device covered, you can add another Ring Protect Basic subscription or upgrade to Ring Protect Plus on Ring.com.

I have a Ring Alarm system. Which Ring Protect Plan is best for me?

We recommend subscribing to Ring Protect Plus, as it provides Assisted Monitoring for your Ring Alarm system.

If I have Ring devices at more than one address, will Ring Protect Plus cover them all?

No. You’ll need to have separate subscriptions to Ring Protect Plus for each address you want to cover.

Can I use multiple phones or tablets with my Ring devices without a subscription to Ring Protect?

Yes. All your Ring products can be viewed and monitored via the Ring mobile app on any mobile phone or tablet, and any PC if you log into your Ring account via Ring.com

What forms of payment are accepted for Ring Protect?

Ring accepts Visa, Mastercard and SEPA, through Ring.com. Ring doesn’t accept any payments in the form of cash, check or money order.

Any payments sent to our mailing address will be returned to you, and you will be instructed to subscribe and pay for Ring Protect online. Payments that are mailed to Ring may cause a lapse in your coverage. If you don’t subscribe and pay for Ring Protect online within your 30-day trial period or prior to the expiration of your existing subscription plan, you may lose your stored video recordings and will not be able to recover them.

If I’m subscribed to Ring Protect Basic or Plus, how do I redeem my 10% discount?

The discount only applies to Ring products on Ring.com. You’ll have to be logged into your Ring account at Ring.com in order for your discount to be applied. If you’re a new subscriber, you’ll need to log out of your account and log back in to use your discount immediately after you subscribe to Ring Protect Plus. See the following question-answer for terms and conditions.

What are the terms of the 10% discount?

Here are the full terms of the discount:

1. The discount applies to neighbours shopping on ring.com with a Ring Protect Basic or Plus subscription at the time of purchase. It does not apply retroactively, meaning you can’t buy a device and then subscribe to Ring Protect Basic or Plus and retroactively apply the discount.
2. The discount cannot be combined with other offers, including limited time offers.
3. The discount is not transferable, may not be resold and has no cash alternative.
4. It cannot be applied to non-Ring products. It applies to all Ring cameras, doorbells, accessories and multipacks.
5. You cannot subscribe to Ring Protect Basic or Plus and use the 10% discount in the same transaction.
6. You must be logged into your account to use the discount at Ring.com during checkout. The discount applies upon the next login session after purchase of Ring Protect Basic or Plus. To use the discount immediately, log out of your account and log back in.
7. The email used to subscribe to your Ring Protect Plan must be used on checkout.
8. We reserve the right to waive enforcement of these terms.

What devices are eligible for extended warranties under Ring Protect Plus?

For extended warranty coverage to apply to your Ring device, the device must be within its original limited warranty period when you subscribe to Ring Protect Plus. Once the original limited warranty expires, the device will be covered under Ring Protect Plus until you discontinue your subscription. Non-Ring and other third-party devices are not eligible for the extended warranty. If you are a consumer, the limited and extended warranties are provided in addition to, and without prejudice to, your consumer rights.

How long are videos stored in my Ring account?

Your videos are stored in your Ring account for up to 180 days if you live in one of the following countries: Andorra, Austria, Belgium, Bulgaria, Croatia, Republic of Cyprus, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Iceland, Ireland, Italy, Latvia, Liechtenstein, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Monaco, Netherlands, Norway, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, UK.

You can choose to delete your videos from your account at any time. Deleted videos cannot be recovered.
