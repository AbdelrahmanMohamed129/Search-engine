TradeCentre - Home of TSN's National Hockey League Trade Deadline Day coverage

NHL

Panthers deal rights to Krygier to Kings

The Florida Panthers announced the trade of the rights to defenceman Cole Kygier to the Los Angeles Kings on Monday. Heading the other way will be future considerations.

NHL

Rangers acquire F Blidh from Avalanche, D Kalynuk from Canucks

The New York Rangers have acquired forward Anton Blidh from the Colorado Avalanche in exchange for forward Gustav Rydahl, the Rangers announced.

NHL

Flames add Stecher, swap Ritchie brothers in deal with Coyotes

The Calgary Flames have acquired defenceman Troy Stecher and forward Nick Ritchie from the Arizona Coyotes in exchange for defenceman Connor Mackey and forward Brett Ritchie.

NHL

Wild acquire C Sundqvist from Red Wings

The Minnesota Wild have acquired centre Oskar Sundqvist from the Detroit Red Wings in exchange for a 2023 fourth round draft pick, the team announced Friday.

NHL

Wild acquire D Klingberg from Ducks

The Anaheim Ducks are trading defenceman John Klingberg to the Minnesota Wild, according to TSN Hockey Insider Pierre LeBrun. Heading back to the Ducks is defenceman Andrej Sustr, the rights to Nikita Nesterenko and a fourth-round pick in 2025.

NHL

Senators send pick to Flyers for F Brown

The Ottawa Senators announced they have acquired forward Patrick Brown from the Philadelphia Flyers in exchange for a sixth-round pick in the 2023 NHL Draft.

NHL

Maple Leafs trade Hunt to Flames, maintain cap space for Murray, Knies

The Toronto Maple Leafs have traded forward Dryden Hunt to the Calgary Flames in exchange for Radim Zohorna, TSN's Darren Dreger reports.

NHL

Wild trade F Greenway to Sabres for picks

The Minnesota Wild have traded forward Jordan Greenway to the Buffalo Sabres for a second and fifth-round pick.

NHL

Pens acquire Kulikov from Ducks

The Pittsburgh Penguins finalized a trade with the Anaheim Ducks for defenceman Dmitry Kulikov in exchange for forward Brock McGinn and a 2024 third-round pick.

NHL

Habs acquire D Allard from Kings for Schnarr

The Montreal Canadiens have acquired defenceman Frédéric Allard from the Los Angeles Kings in exchange for forward Nate Schnarr.

NHL

Canucks deal Lazar to Devils, Kalynuk to Rangers

The New Jersey Devils are acquiring forward Curtis Lazar from the Vancouver Canucks in exchange for a fourth-round pick in 2024.

NHL

Sharks deal recently-acquired F Namestnikov to Jets

Vladislav Namestnikov has been traded from the San Jose Sharks to the Winnipeg Jets for a fourth-round pick in 2025.

NHL

Kings acquire MacEwen from Flyers for Lemieux

The Los Angeles Kings are sending forward Brendan Lemieux to the Philadelphia Flyers in exchange for forward Zack MacEwen. The Flyers are also receiving a fifth-round pick in the 2024 NHL Draft.

NHL

Blues acquire Vrana from Red Wings

The St. Louis Blues have acquired Jakub Vrana from the Detroit Red Wings in exchange for a seventh-round pick in the 2025 NHL Draft and forward Dylan McLaughlin

NHL

Penguins add Bonino in three-team trade with Sharks, Habs

Nick Bonino is returning to the Pittsburgh Penguins. The San Jose Sharks are trading the veteran centre to Pittsburgh for fifth- and seventh-round picks, TSN Hockey Insider Pierre LeBrun reports.

NHL

Stars acquire F Domi from Blackhawks

The Chicago Blackhawks have traded forward Max Domi and goaltender Dylan Wells to the Dallas Stars for goaltender Anton Khudobin and a second-round draft pick.

NHL

Oilers acquire F Bjugstad, D Dineen from Coyotes for prospect, third-round pick

The Edmonton Oilers announced they have acquired forward Nick Bjugstad and defenceman Cam Dinee from the Arizona Coyotes in exchange for prospect Michael Kesselring and a third-round pick.

NHL

Blackhawks acquire Bjork from Sabres

The Chicago Blackhawks are acquiring forward Anders Bjork from the Buffalo Sabres in exchange for future considerations, it was announced Thursday.

NHL

Senators land Chychrun from Coyotes

The Ottawa Senators have landed Jakob Chychrun in a deal with the Arizona Coyotes. Heading back to the Coyotes are a 2023 conditional first-round pick, a conditional second-round pick in 2024 that originally belonged to the Washington Capitals and Ottawa's own second-round pick in 2026.

NHL

Blue Jackets trade injured F Voracek, pick to Coyotes for Gillies

Columbus Blue Jackets traded injured forward Jakub Voracek to the Arizona Coyotes on Thursday, shedding his $8.25 million cap hit through next season. The Blue Jackets sent a sixth-round pick along with Voracek, whose NHL future is in doubt, to Arizona in the deal.

NHL

Bruins acquire pending UFA Bertuzzi from Red Wings

The Detroit Red Wings are trading winger Tyler Bertuzzi to the Boston Bruins. Bertuzzi, 28, has four goals and 14 points while being limited to 29 games this season due to injury. He carries a $4.75 million cap hit.

NHL

Kings trade Quick, two picks to Blue Jackets in deal for Gavrikov, Korpisalo

The Los Angeles Kings are trading goaltender Jonathan Quick to the Columbus Blue Jackets as part of a deal for defenceman Vladislav Gavrikov and goaltender Joonas Korpisalo. A 2023 first- and 2024 third-round pick are also heading to Columbus in the trade.

NHL

Avalanche acquire F Eller from Capitals

The Washington Capitals have traded forward Lars Eller to the Colorado Avalanche in exchange for a 2025 second-round pick. Eller has seven goals and 16 points in 60 games this season. He is a pending unrestricted free agent, carrying a cap hit of $3.5 million.

NHL

Golden Knights acquire F Blueger from Penguins

The Pittsburgh Penguins have traded forward Teddy Blueger to the Vegas Golden Knights in exchange for defenceman Peter DiLiberatore and a 2024 third-round draft pick, the team announced on Wednesday.

NHL

Rangers acquire F Kane from Blackhawks in three-team trade involving Coyotes

The New York Rangers acquired Patrick Kane in a trade with the Chicago Blackhawks on Tuesday, reuniting the star winger with former teammate Artemi Panarin and stamping themselves as a Stanley Cup contender in the loaded Eastern Conference.

Oilers land Ekholm from Predators

The Edmonton Oilers stayed busy on Tuesday, acquiring defenceman Mattias Ekholm from the Nashville Predators in exchange for defenceman Tyson Barrie, forward Reid Schaefer, a 2023 first-round pick and a fourth-round selection in 2024.

NHL

Oilers trade Puljujarvi to Hurricanes for Puistola

The Edmonton Oilers have traded Jesse Puljujarvi to the Carolina Hurricanes in exchange for Patrik Puistola.
