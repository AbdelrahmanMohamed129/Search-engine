How calls work

Everything is in your hands

A Be My Eyes call pops up on your phone as a push notification. Simply tap it to answer the call. If you are not able to answer, another volunteer will answer.
Once on the call, you can see through their rear camera. The caller canâ€™t see you but you can hear each other.
The caller points their camera towards what they need support seeing. All you have to do is to act as their eyes and tell the caller what you see through their phone camera.
After the call you hang up, rate the call and wait for the next incoming call.
You receive a push notification about incoming calls.

What languages are supported?

The primary language offered for Corporate Volunteering events is English although additional languages are available. Â Please reach out to your Be My Eyes Representative or use the form below to request additional languages as we want to ensure a great experience for all participants.

Does corporate volunteering events work across different time zones?

Yes. Just make sure that when the event runs that the local time is between 8am and 9pm in the other timezones. By default no one gets calls at night time between 9pm to 8am.

What are the typical tasks the callers need support with?

Itâ€™s usually everyday tasks like distinguishing items, reading small print, setting the washer etc. A call usually doesnâ€™t last more than a minute or 2 but occasionally it can be more.

How long can events be?

Corporate volunteering events can last up to 24 hours. Depending on your situation you can do a focused timeslot of e.g. 2 hours where we will prioritize calls to your team. You can also have the event span e.g. 8 hours and do it in between your employeesâ€™ work schedules. Depending on the chosen timeframe we adjust the frequency of incoming calls accordingly.

What if we canâ€™t answer all incoming calls?

Fear not. We actually notify 20 people of a call, so donâ€™t worry if you are unable to answer. We do this so that no call goes unanswered. All calls are on average answered in less than 20 seconds.

We want to make this an annual event. Can we do that?

Sure! Please reach out to us directly using the form below and we are happy to answer further questions and tailor the annual events to your needs.

If you are looking for a one-time CV event, please select the number of participants for the event at the top of this page.
